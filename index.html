The New BMW M5

Unleash the spirit of the race track with the luxury of a grand tourer. The **BMW M5** is the perfect fusion of dynamic performance and exclusive style.

Explore the M5

Design: An Expression of Power

Aerodynamic Profile

Every line and curve of the **BMW M5** is sculpted for performance. The long, sweeping hood, the aggressive front apron, and the subtle spoiler all work in harmony to reduce drag and enhance downforce, ensuring exceptional stability at high speeds.

Iconic Grille and Lights

The signature kidney grille with its double-bar design and the sleek, powerful laser headlights give the **M5** an unmistakable and commanding presence. These elements are not just for show; they optimize airflow for superior engine cooling.

Performance: Power Unleashed

TwinPower Turbo V8 Engine

At the core of the **M5** is a 4.4-liter TwinPower Turbo V8 engine that delivers breathtaking power and a thrilling soundtrack. This engine is a masterpiece of engineering, offering incredible responsiveness and a high-revving character.

  • Horsepower: 617 hp
  • Torque: 553 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 3.0 seconds

Interior: Crafted for the Driver

M Sport Seats

Ergonomically designed M Sport seats with integrated headrests and illuminated **M5** logos provide exceptional lateral support and long-distance comfort.

Driver-Focused Cockpit

The entire cabin is oriented towards the driver, with a perfectly positioned M steering wheel, digital instrument cluster, and head-up display that keeps all vital information in your line of sight.

Premium Materials

The **M5**'s interior is adorned with the finest materials, from sumptuous Merino leather upholstery to carbon fiber trim, creating an atmosphere of pure, unadulterated luxury.

Technology: Innovation at Your Fingertips

BMW iDrive System

The **M5** features the latest BMW iDrive system with a large, high-resolution touchscreen display, intuitive controls, and advanced voice recognition. Seamlessly integrate your digital life with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

Driver Assistance Systems

Equipped with a suite of cutting-edge safety and driver-assistance features, the **M5** offers peace of mind on every journey. This includes adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and a surround-view camera system.
